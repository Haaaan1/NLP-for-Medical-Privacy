About This Tool

This medical data anonymization platform leverages state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to protect sensitive information in unstructured clinical texts, supporting both English and German languages.

This tool is designed for the thesis work "Developing a Natural Language Processing Tool for Enhanced Privacy Protection in Medical Data " done at Communication Systems Group, University of Zurich, supervised by Mr. Weijie Niu, Dr. Alberto Huertas Celdran and Prof. Dr. Burkhard Stiller.

Key Features

  • Advanced Entity Recognition: Utilizes a high-precision Medical-NER model (85%+ accuracy for English, 75%+ for German) to identify sensitive patient information
  • Flexible De-identification: Offers multiple protection strategies:
    • Deletion (complete redaction)
    • Pseudonymization (label-based replacement)
    • Generalization (for Age/Date with adjustable precision levels)
  • Privacy Risk Assessment: Computes weighted risk scores before and after processing to demonstrate effectiveness
  • Multiple Input Formats: Accepts both plain text and CSV file uploads
